The Arabic language is a living testament to the human imagination. It is the official language for 500 M people, and a medium for spiritual, theological and legal inspirations for more than 1.5 Bn people.

Its script inspired, and still adorns, some of the world’s greatest architectural marvels such as Taj Mahal and Alhambra palace, all while preserving the words of Rumi, Hafez and Gibran. A tongue for mystics, scholars, and a script with calligraphic beauty and precision.

Yet in the race to develop universal AGI, its datasets are the one of the most lacking and currently trivialized. Madena AI bridges this gap, employing physicists, computer scientists, linguists and lawyers from a diverse background of native arabic speakers to develop the next generation data, evaluations and models for arabic.
